About Us

Club De Théâtre is another prominent club of the college. It is the official drama club of IIIT NR which encourages dramatic presentation by students in form of skits, mime, plays, nukkad natak, etc. The club works well to bring the culture of drama, comedy, stand-up and other relevant stuff into the institute. The aim of this club is to explore, participate and learn as much as we can.
