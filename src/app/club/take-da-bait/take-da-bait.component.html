About Us

‘Take da bait’ is an idiom to lure someone to do or say exactly what one intends to! In continuous pronunciation it sounds as if ‘Take Debate’. The first part of the statement might seem greedy and quite negative if and not if, the only purpose of this group is to invite people to facts, help them form their own set of opinions and most majorly listen to what each IIITian feels about most of the ongoing trends and current issues in the world. This cultural club was officially sanctioned under SAC on 5th January 2020.

The necessity of a debate club like this one is not limited to offering facts and ideas, it’s also to hear out each-other’s opinion and strengthen our own in the process. The bait is the world trends here, all the current affairs which are raining down on us as mere headlines on random newspapers or social media sites. Technical knowledge probably won’t help us connect to the changing socio-political environment of the world. The technology IIITians would develop could probably be majorly then blindfolded of advantages or disadvantages that the world presents to us. To prevent such hindrances to a bright future, ”Take da bait” officially took charge. Every small or big change discussed in the club's debate forums improves us not only as citizens and students but also as future developers and leaders of tomorrow.
