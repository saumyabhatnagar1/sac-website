About Us

This club is basically works for the betterment of the society. So, we have the following activities:
(1) Inviting representatives from NGOs, the government, and scientific and research fields to identify pain points that require technological interventions / solutions
(2) Visiting local villages for on-ground assessment and interacting with local villages
(3) Executing the identified projects
(4) Updating ourselves on innovation happening around the world and taking up potentially impactful projects

The purpose of joining this club is worth as follows:
(1) There is great satisfaction in doing our bit for the underprivileged and the society at large
(2) Our club activities expose our members to real life problems
(3) They encourage us to gain valuable technical skills and eventually, attain adeptness in them
(4) Members receive mentorship from innovators, industry and research partners and collaborating faculty

It is the meeting ground for various types of individuals - regardless of whether their objective is to help others, receive valuable mentorship, grow as a person, or simply develop their skills.

Tech4Gud is a club that embodies the aspirations and the ambition of the student ambitions.
