About Us

The club was founded in the Autumn Semester 2018, where it was previously named as CECoT (Community of electronics and communication techies) in the month of October and was later renamed as Community of Electronics. The club was established with the core founding members as Harsh Singh, Akhil Kumar, Jatin Aditya, Abhay Sao and Gargi Shukla and mentored by Dr. Debanjan Das (Asst. Prof., ECE) at IIIT-NR. The team however was later on extended, keeping in mind of the requirements and objectives of the club.

Our main objective is to bring together like minded people and try to find solutions to the real world problems using the resources we have. The club, encourages its members to participate in ideathons and bring out new and creative ideas.
