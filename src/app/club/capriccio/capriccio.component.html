About Us

Capriccio club boons people to lose themselves in the music. It comes from melodies and tunes strung together by people who sing and play musical instruments. It is not enough to just play the notes, or sing the words, or hear the songs. For it to mean something, you have to feel the music. Because if we don't feel it, how can anyone else? Once you hear the magical sound of music, you instantly feel harmony inside your heart and if it is good, you dance!

Music is the art or science of combining vocal or instrumental sounds (sometimes both) to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion. Songs are the best companion of one's life. We can make the most of our voyage with the listening of music.
