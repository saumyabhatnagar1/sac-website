About Us

CipherCell is an official cybersecurity club of IIIT-NR. This club intends to bring general awareness about hacking and to break the stigma surrounding the field, meanwhile educating those who are interested in cybersecurity. We mostly ignore the fact that the only thing brings order to our society that thrives on the internet is cybersecurity. Without proper security protocols, the banks, management systems, and navigation will crumble within seconds. Still, there are many misconceptions surrounding the field, while some are intimidated by the field, others simply frown upon the issue.

We live in a world which is too good for its own good, everybody living in their own versions of reality and justifying their existence. It’s surely a good time to be alive, while some use the technology to deduce new solutions and make our daily life simple most of them are just prying on the innocents and robbing of their money.

We all think that why would someone try to hack us? Like we don’t work in intelligence services and nor do we have an astronomical amount of wealth.

But the thing that we all forget is that Data is the new oil. If we own someone’s data we can use it in many destructive manners. We can gain leverage on someone or even worse we could compromise the whole organization.

Nuclear Attacks are overrated, think of an ultimate weapon which can bring us all to stone-age. An attack which can rewrite your identity or completely erase you from the system, sounds scary right? Because it is.

Stay aware, stay safe.
