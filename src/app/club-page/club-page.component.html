The Society of Coders

About Us

The Society of Coders is IIIT-Naya Raipur's official coding club. Started by students in a bid to spur the coding culture at this fledging college, the club is responsible for hosting and conducting a diverse range of coding events - hackathons, workshops, coding competitions and much more. We aim to acquaint students with the various facets of coding, with special emphasis on doing things rather than learning things. It also serves as a forum for people to share their ideas, thoughts and ambitions regarding programming in an couraging and conducive environment. It's a club which welcomes everyone from first time coders to experienced veterans of the trade.
