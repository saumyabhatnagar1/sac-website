About The Hackathon

Hack-a-Sol is the annual hackathon organised by The Society of Coders, IIIT Naya Raipur. The participants are given some problem statements and within 24 hours they have to come up with a solution.

  • Cash Prizes for Winners
  • Participation Certificate
  • Get Inspired by Amazing Speakers
