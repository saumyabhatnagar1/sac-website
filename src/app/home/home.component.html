About SAC

SAC (Students' Activity Center) was established in the year 2018 and it aims to promote and support student activities in IIIT Naya Raipur. Under SAC, all sorts of activities including technical, cultural, sports, arts, science, student affairs, etc. are managed. Currently, SAC has two UG Representatives; two Science and Technology Representatives; two Arts, Cultural and Literary Respresentative; and two Sports Representatives. All working under the President of SAC, who is a faculty mentor.
