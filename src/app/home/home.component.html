About SAC

SAC (Students' Activity Center) was established in the year 2018 and it aims to promote and support student activities in IIIT Naya Raipur. Under SAC, all sorts of activities including technical, cultural, sports, arts, science, student affairs, etc. are managed.
Currently, SAC has 2 UG Representatives; 2 Science and Technology Representatives; 2 Arts, Cultural and Literary Respresentative; and 2 Sports Representatives.
All working under the President of SAC, who is a faculty mentor.
