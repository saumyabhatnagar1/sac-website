About Us

HACK-A-SOL is a pan India Hackathon organized by Technical clubs of IIIT Naya Raipur in collaboration with SAC . It is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new software program. The word hackathon is a portmanteau of the words hacker, which means clever programmer, and marathon, an event marked by endurance. The topic of this event range from AIML to Bloackchain. GET! SET!
